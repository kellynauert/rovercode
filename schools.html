Rovercode for Schools

Designed for the Virtual and Physical Classroom

Easy to teach

Any teacher, regardless of experience, can teach early computer science with Rovercode. Rovercode's instructional lesson plans guide teachers through facilitating, debugging, and scheduling each module, and the block-coding interface simplifies the coding process, allowing students and teachers to focus on logic instead of syntax.

Adaptive content

Rovercode's curriculum is continuously being improved in order to adapt to different student experience levels and needs.

Standards aligned

Rovercode is standards aligned and satisfies national and state early-CS requirements, making it a complete solution to early-CS requirements.
